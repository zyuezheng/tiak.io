About Us

We are a young and vibrant startup which focuses on App Development and Internet of Things. We have a total of more than 20 years experience in the IT industry.

We develop technology services and products on contactless smart access control. Tiak helps to integrate smart door access to existing system and simplify your access management processes.

Bluetooth/Wi-Fi

Uses Bluetooth 5.0 and Wi-Fi communication to unlock doors within proximity or from anywhere if you are connected to the internet.

Smart Access

Digital access gives you more control and power to manage your door locks, add or renew cards and give access to the right people at the right time.

Tracking and Data Analysis

Gives you the ability to analyse access timings and movements around the facility. Analysis can be done more easily with the data recorded for you.
